Brand Design · 2021

Smart Point

Built to be seen. Designed to be trusted.

01 — The Brief

Smart Point needed a visual identity that reflected the reliability and professionalism of its repair services. I created a cohesive branding system spanning storefront graphics, indoor signage, stationery, packaging, and promotional materials.

02 — The Challenge

The brand lacked a consistent visual presence across customer touchpoints. The goal was to create a recognizable identity that would stand out on the street while building trust with customers.

03 — The Approach

I developed a unified design system built around consistent colors, typography, shapes, and visual language. The project included large-format storefront banners, indoor entrance graphics, business cards, gift boxes, and branded collateral. Every element was designed to work together, creating a seamless experience from first impression to final purchase.
